## Free (Lite): $0/mo

Full product, no account required.

What you get:
- up to **3 devices**
- direct P2P sync (QUIC via iroh, encrypted)
- files up to **10 MB**
- N0 public relay (4 global regions, free)
- Tailscale / WireGuard / VPN — automatic, no config
- mDNS local network auto-discovery
- 6-digit pair codes — no account needed
- clipboard history (24h)
- auto-update
- **works without an account** — use on local network only

What you don't get:
- central relay (relay.clipshot.cc)
- 30-day clipboard history

What this looks like in the UI:
- sidebar badge says **Lite** (or **Local** without account)
- Settings shows peer limit and relay status
- 4th+ device appears as limited — click **Activate** to swap it in

## Pro: $5/mo

Pro removes the device cap and adds enterprise features.

What you get:
- **unlimited devices**
- files up to **200 MB**
- central relay transport (sync through any NAT/firewall)
- 30-day clipboard history
- priority support
- everything in Free

## Lifetime Pro: $100 one-time

Pay once, sync forever. All Pro features with no recurring payments.

What you get:
- everything in Pro
- no recurring payments
- lifetime updates

## Grace period

If your Pro subscription was recently confirmed by Clipshot Portal, Clipshot keeps Pro features for a **14-day grace period** even if the hub is temporarily unreachable.
